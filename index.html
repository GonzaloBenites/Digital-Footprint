Define the term: Digital Footprint

Digital fingerprint is the unique information generated by a person's online activities, such as their browsing history, social media behavior, online purchases and other digital data that can be used to identify, track or authenticate a user online.

Tips for taking care of your digital footprint

To protect your digital footprint, it's crucial to control the personal information you share online, adjust the privacy settings on your accounts, use strong passwords, and keep your devices up-to-date with security software.

Drawbacks

The drawbacks are that biometric data is compromised or improperly accessed, it is difficult to remedy the situation since they cannot be changed like passwords. Additionally, the reliability of biometric identification can be affected by factors such as the quality of the scan, the physical variability of the individual, or even the possibility of fingerprint falsification.
